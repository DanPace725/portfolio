About Me

I am a B.S. Psychology student at Southern Oregon University with a strong focus on the intersection of cognitive science, AI, and decision-making. My research explores how information environments structure human agency and how we can design better interfaces for neurodivergent minds.

I combine a background in psychology with technical skills in Next.js, Python, and data analysis to build tools that support human cognition, such as the Brain Explorer and the ACE Application.
